the Insider - Privacy Policy

The 'the Insider' services and mobile app (collectively, the “Services”) offer users a forum to interact with other 'the Insider' users in the same organization, in order to post and share comments, and to respond to comments posted by others. In this Privacy Policy (“Policy”), we describe how we collect, use, and disclose information that we obtain about users of our Services, which are available via mobile applications offered in the Apple and Android app stores. Your use of our Services, and any dispute over privacy, is subject to this Policy and our Terms of Service, including its applicable limitations on damages and the resolution of disputes, which is available at [http://www.theinsider.cc/Privacy.html] and incorporated by reference into this Policy.

Invite Contacts

You can choose to “Share the Insider” by inviting contacts via email, text or other means. You choose what contacts to invite and when to invite them. If you invite a contact, we will generate a message with the 'the Insider' download URL included, which you can choose to send via your mobile device.

Your Posts

You can delete content you have posted (including replies). Content you delete will be hidden from other users. However, 'the Insider' may maintain a copy of such content in our records.

Push Notifications/Alerts

We may send push notifications or alerts to your device. You can deactivate these messages at any time by changing the notification settings on your device or by changing your 'the Insider' settings.

Third Party Analytics

We may use automated devices and applications, included those offered by third parties to evaluate usage of our Services. We also may use other analytic means to evaluate our Services. We use these tools to help us improve our Services, performance and user experiences. We do not share your personal information with these third parties.

Third Party Ads

We may display ads to you (including in-app ads), and we may target those ads based on your location, your 'the Insider' activities, and other criteria (e.g., the time of day).

Third-Party Links

Our Services may contain links to third-party websites. Any access to and use of such linked websites is not governed by this Policy, but instead is governed by the privacy policies of those third party websites.

Minors

You must be at least 17 to use our Services. Our Services are not designed for children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information from our systems.

Contact Us

If you have questions about the privacy aspects of our Services or would like to make a complaint, please contact us at [support@theinsider.cc].

Changes to this Policy

This Policy is current as of the Effective Date set forth above. We may change this Policy from time to time, so please be sure to check back periodically. We will post any changes to this Policy on our Site, at [http://www.theinsider.cc/Privacy.html]. If we make any changes to this Policy that materially affect our practices with regard to the personal information we have previously collected from you, we will endeavor to provide you with notice in advance of such change by highlighting the change on our Site, or providing notice in our mobile app.
